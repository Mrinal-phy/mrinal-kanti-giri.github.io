Research identity

I use analytical methods, tensor-network techniques, and quantum circuit design to explore novel quantum phenomena and develop efficient quantum algorithms.

At Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, I work on digital quantum simulations and quantum circuit optimization for IBM superconducting hardware.

My research interests span quantum transport, localization phenomena, topological properties, and robust algorithms for noisy intermediate-scale quantum devices.
